Quality Education for Every Ghanaian Student

Access free educational resources, interactive courses, and exam preparation materials designed specifically for Ghana's curriculum. Join thousands of students and teachers from all walks of life.

Our Mission

To provide free, high-quality educational resources to every Ghanaian student, regardless of their location or economic background, empowering them to achieve academic excellence.

Our Vision

To become Ghana's leading digital education platform, bridging the educational gap and creating equal learning opportunities for all students across the country.

Our Values

Accessibility, Quality, Innovation, Community, and Empowerment - guiding everything we do to transform education in Ghana.

Our Story

EduPortal Ghana was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate educators, technologists, and former students who recognized the growing need for accessible digital learning resources in Ghana.

Starting as a small initiative to share WASSCE past questions online, we've grown into a comprehensive educational platform serving thousands of students across all 16 regions of Ghana.

Today, we collaborate with experienced Ghanaian teachers, curriculum experts, and educational institutions to develop content that truly meets the needs of Ghanaian students.
